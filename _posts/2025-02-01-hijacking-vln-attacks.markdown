    <p align="justify">
      We study adversarial attacks on Vision-and-Language Navigation (VLN) agents through localized modifications in the
      agentâ€™s operating environment. We develop a white-box environmental attack that optimizes the appearance of a 3D
      attack object to induce targeted behaviors in pretrained VLN agents. The attack can cause agents to ignore instructions,
      terminate early, or follow an attacker-defined trajectory, and generalizes to novel instructions and paths not used during optimization.
    </p>
