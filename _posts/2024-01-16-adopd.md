    <p align="justify">
      Research in document image understanding is hindered by limited high-quality document data.
      ADOPD introduces a large-scale dataset for document page decomposition with a data-driven
      document taxonomy discovery process during collection, and dense annotations.
      It supports four tasks: Doc2Mask, Doc2Box, Doc2Tag, and Doc2Seq.
    </p>

    <ul>
      <li><b>Data-driven taxonomy discovery</b> during collection to improve diversity and balance.</li>
      <li><b>Dense annotations</b> including entity masks and text bounding boxes; plus tags/captions cleaned with human involvement.</li>
      <li><b>Four benchmark tasks</b>: Doc2Mask / Doc2Box / Doc2Tag / Doc2Seq.</li>
    </ul>
